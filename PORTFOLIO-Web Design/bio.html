Bio

I'm a designer and creative with a passion for blending storytelling, design, and user experience. I'm fascinated by history, believing it guides how we evolve and shapes the future of design. I’m currently focused on building a brutalist-inspired website that functions like a digital zine—combining raw, minimal aesthetics with intuitive UX.

One of my favorite pieces of advice comes from Ralph Waldo Emerson: "Nature is upheld by antagonism. Passions, resistance, danger, are educators. We acquire the strength we have overcome." It reminds me that great design, like nature, often emerges from tension and challenges. I’m always seeking new ways to bring fresh ideas to life while remaining grounded in timeless principles.
